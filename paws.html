PAWS Animal Shelter 2019

My coworkers and I volunteered at PAWS Chicago Animal Shelter.

We cleaned, organized shipment of donated animal food, and met some adorable cats and dogs.

PAWS' mission is to:

  • Build No Kill communities that respect and respect and value the life of every cat and dog.
  • End the overpopulation of homeless animals through solutions, practices, and education.
  • Transform animal welfare by setting higher standards in the way animals are treated and developing a sustainable, solutions-based model.

Breeds of cats available for fostering are:

  • Domestic Shorthair
  • Domestic Medium Hair
  • Domestic Longhair

Ranges from kitten to core foster to advanced foster

Breeds of dogs available for adoption are:

  • Boxer Mix
  • Retriever, Labrador/Mix
  • Mountain Cur/Mix
  • Jack Russell Terrier Mix
  • Chihuahua/Mix
  • Pug/Beagle
  • Hound/Shepherd Mix or Hound/Mix
  • Pinscher, Miniature/Mix
  • Shepherd or Shepherd Mix
  • And more!

Ranges from all ages

"EVERY LIFE COUNTS."

"Once safe at PAWS Chicago, every pet is treated as an individual and guaranteed life, no matter where they come from and what they need." - PAWS Chicago
